## Pattern Relationships

Patterns are interconnected through:
- **Sequences**: Groups of related patterns that flow together
- **Scale**: Patterns at different scales (towns → buildings → construction)
- **Preceding/Following**: Context patterns and refinement patterns
- **Category**: Shared organizational grouping
- **Emergence**: Synergies that arise from pattern combinations

Use the hypergraph in `opencog_atomese/` to explore these relationships programmatically.
